# Table: hero_power

**Database**: ods

## Columns

| Column | Alias | Type | Description |
|--------|-------|------|-------------|
| hero_id | hero id | integer | the id of the hero
Maps to superhero(id) |
| power_id | power id | integer | the id of the power
Maps to superpower(id) |

## Business Logic & Value Descriptions

### power_id (power id)

- commonsense evidence:
- In general, a superhero's attributes provide the foundation for their abilities and help to define who they are, while their powers are the specific abilities that they use to fight crime and protect others.
